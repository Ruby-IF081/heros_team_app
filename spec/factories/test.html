SoftServe volunteers joined the Hour of Code in Lviv

12 Dec2017 The Hour of Code was held in the Lviv schools. This is a global educational movement aimed at promoting informational technologies. Within the framework of the project, IT specialists attend educational institutions they conduct a one-hour introduction to computer science together with teachers. Thanks to the developed materials and the support of experienced mentors, children learn to code in the format of a game. The initiative was founded in 2013 by the organization Code.org. Since its foundation, it has reached over 480 million schoolchildren in 180+ countries worldwide. Read more
